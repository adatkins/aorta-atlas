Prototype notes

This first build is intentionally structured as a decision-support interface rather than a simple calculator card.

This prototype is for clinician-directed decision support and product design review. It should not replace specialist judgment, source-document review, or institutional protocols. Additional rules can be layered into the same engine for more complete guideline coverage, imaging pathways, surveillance cadence, acute syndromes, and operative strategy modules.
